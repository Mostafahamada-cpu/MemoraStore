Our Story

How It Began

They say the best stories start when you least expect them. For Omar and Rowan, that moment arrived quietly — an ordinary conversation that was supposed to last five minutes and stretched into hours, the kind that made the rest of the world go soft at the edges.

What followed was a friendship that deepened into something neither of them wanted to rush, built on long walks, shared playlists, and the particular comfort of being fully understood by someone else.

When Omar asked Rowan to spend forever together, there was really only one answer. Now, surrounded by the people who love them most, they're ready to begin the next chapter — as partners, as a family, as each other's favorite person.
